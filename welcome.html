Finding the house

Getting here.

Casa Rei Azul is at 51–53 Rua Diogo de Mendonça Corte Real, Olhão, Portugal.

From Faro Airport, the journey normally takes around 15 minutes by car.

Uber works very well in the Algarve and is usually the easiest way to reach the house from the airport.

Coming inside

When you step through the blue front doors, head straight upstairs.

You’ll arrive at the living room, two bedrooms, two bathrooms and the log burner.

One more flight of stairs takes you to the kitchen, TV area and terrace.

One final staircase leads to the rooftop.

That’s our favourite place in the house — especially around sunset.

If we were spending three days in Olhão

A simple plan.

Day one

Morning

Walk to the waterfront, find coffee, and explore the market.

Afternoon

Settle into the house, pick up supplies, and enjoy the terrace.

Evening

Open the wine on the rooftop, then walk into town for dinner.

Day two

Morning

Take the boat into the Ria Formosa.

Lunch

Choose Armona, Culatra or Farol for seafood by the water.

Evening

Return slowly to Olhão and keep the evening simple.

Day three

Morning

Visit Faro or Tavira for a change of scenery.

Afternoon

Come back to the house for a quiet hour on the terrace.

Evening

Final dinner in town, or a BBQ upstairs if you feel like staying in.

Have a wonderful stay.
